The Fox’s Anvil · Open Resilience Tools

The Fox’s Anvil is a commons-based workshop for tools that meet the world at the scale of land, water, and community. These are not wearables or toys. They are open designs for real work: restoration, flood and fire response, agroecology, and community resilience.

All designs shared here are part of an open hardware commons. They are meant to be built, repaired, adapted, and improved by farmers, welders, mechanics, neighbors, students, and volunteers—anyone willing to take responsibility for their use.

Relation to MythWear

MythWear Commons focuses on body-scale tools, garments, and movement companions. The Fox’s Anvil focuses on land-scale and infrastructure-scale tools. They are siblings: one close to the skin, one close to the ground.

For somatic and wearable tools, visit: MythWear Commons .

Shared in the spirit of the Commons. No patents, no enclosure. Build with care. Use with responsibility. Share improvements.
